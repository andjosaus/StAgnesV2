Your Journey to "I Do!"

Preparing for your marriage is one of the most exciting times in your life. Here's a simple guide to the process.

1. Let's Set a Date!

Contact the parish office (ideally 8-12 months out) to chat about your desired date and get the ball rolling.

2. Meet & Greet

You'll have a friendly, casual meeting with Fr. James to talk about your journey, the sacrament, and any questions.

3. The Prep Weekend

Join other couples for an engaging weekend of growth and fun. (Next Session: Mar 27-28, 2026)

4. Plan Your Celebration

Meet again to choose the sacred readings and music that make your wedding liturgy uniquely *yours*.

Your Weekend at a Glance

How far in advance should we really contact the parish?

We recommend 8 to 12 months before your wedding. This allows ample time for the preparation process without any rush and helps ensure your preferred date is available.

What if one of us isn't Catholic?

This is very common and welcome! A marriage between a Catholic and a baptized non-Catholic (or non-baptized person) is absolutely possible. Fr. James will discuss this with you during your initial meeting to ensure all your questions are answered.

What does the $120 fee cover?

The $120 fee for the Marriage Preparation Weekend covers all your materials, books, as well as all food and refreshments throughout the weekend. There may be a separate stipend for the church and musician, which you can discuss with the parish office.

Can we do our preparation here if we marry elsewhere?

Yes! As long as you are getting married at a Catholic church in Northwestern Ontario you can complete your marriage prep course at St. Agnes.
